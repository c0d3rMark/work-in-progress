What is Content Management Software?

Content management software allows you to easily create, control and deliver content across all the signage in your network. It creates a central point of control, allowing you to monitor, access and modify the content that is displayed. Content management software ensures your signage is relevant, localized and personal. It allows you to quickly create content with pre-loaded templates to customize and alter the message as you please. From there, deploying your content is easy with the drag and drop interface.
Content management software allows you to deliver specific content to a single location or to all locations in your network. Your digital signage will be able to target and engage audiences like never before. You'll have full control of your digital signage to display what you want, when you want!
